Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Information We Collect

We may collect personal details you share with us when you make a booking, submit an enquiry, contact us by email or WhatsApp, or subscribe to updates.

This can include your name, phone number, email address, booking dates, room preferences, and any special requests you provide.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to respond to enquiries, process booking requests, communicate about your stay, and improve our guest services.

If you choose to receive updates, we may also use your contact information to send promotions or important service announcements.

Data Protection

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure. Access to guest information is limited to people who need it to support bookings and operations.

Third-Party Services

Our website may link to third-party services such as Google Maps, WhatsApp, Facebook, and TripAdvisor. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy or how your information is handled, please contact Monte Carlo Guesthouse at montecarloguesthouse@gmail.com or call 0819557777.
