Our Journey

The Monte Carlo Story

Founded in 2010, Monte Carlo Guesthouse began as a vision to create a sanctuary that blends luxury with authentic Namibian hospitality. What started as a family home transformed into a premier accommodation destination in Eehnana.

Over the years, we've carefully expanded and refined our offerings, always guided by our core principle: to provide guests with an unforgettable experience that combines modern comfort with the warm, welcoming spirit of Namibia.

Today, Monte Carlo Guesthouse stands as a testament to sustainable luxury tourism, recognized for our commitment to excellence and our deep connection to the local community and environment.
