Terms and Conditions

Booking requests are subject to availability and are only confirmed once Monte Carlo Guesthouse has reviewed the request and contacted the guest.

Guests are responsible for providing accurate contact details and notifying us promptly of any changes to arrival dates, occupancy, or special needs.

Cancellation Policy

Unless otherwise agreed in writing, cancellations made up to 48 hours before arrival may be accepted without penalty. Late cancellations and no-shows may be subject to charges.

Group bookings, peak-season reservations, or special offers may have separate cancellation terms, which will be shared during the booking process.

Standard check-in and check-out times apply unless another arrangement has been confirmed. Guests are expected to respect property rules, safety guidance, and the comfort of other guests during their stay.
